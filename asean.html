Association of Southeast Asian Nations
(ASEAN)

Member states in Southeast Asia make up the Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN), a regional intergovernmental organization. Since its founding in 1967, ASEAN has worked to advance regional integration and collaboration, boost social and economic advancement, and uphold regional peace and stability. You will get the chance to represent a member state in the ASEAN committee at the Model UN and participate in dynamic discussions and negotiations to solve issues pertaining to social justice, economic development, and regional security. The ASEAN committee offers delegates a distinctive and interesting opportunity to learn about the issues affecting Southeast Asia and hone their leadership, critical thinking, and public speaking abilities. These issues range from combating transnational crime to supporting sustainable development.

This committee is open to delegates of all skills, welcoming 20 delegates to participate in fruitful debate. Position Papers are mandatory for this committee.

Edward Liu

Director

A senior at The Woodlands Secondary School, Edward is thrilled to be able to serve as the Director of the Association of South East Asian Nations committee at BCMUN 2023. Edward's journey with Model UN began in grade 9, and he has been passionately involved in both participating in and organizing Model UN conferences ever since, serving on the Secretariat teams at both TMUN and CAIMUN. His fondest moments in Model UN involve meeting new people and making friends, and he is eager to provide delegates with similar experiences. Beyond the world of MUN, Edward enjoys playing strategy games on his computer and speed-walking around Mississauga. Edward is excited to meet everyone at BCMUN 2023, and he wishes all delegates the best of luck.

Richard Xue

Assistant Director

Richard is a student hailing from Dr. EP Scarlett High School. With a keen interest in international affairs, he eagerly takes on the role of Assistant Director for the ASEAN Committee at BCMUN 2023. Reflecting upon his years in global affairs and politics, from his very first stuttering speech to confidently speaking at multiple Model UN conferences, he continues to be shaped by his continuous journeys in the world of MUN. Outside of the Model United Nations community, Richard also has a strong affinity for chess and enjoys contemplating his blunders on the checkerboard and finds relaxation and entertainment in watching the television series "Young Sheldon".
